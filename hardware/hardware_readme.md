# Hardware - Mechanical Assembly
## Manufacturing of Parts
Majority of linkages and parts are 3D printed and connected through aluminum 2 mm dowels and elastics.
Post processing (sanding, drilling, etc.) of the 3D printed parts was neccessary for smooth rotation and interaction of parts and joints.
